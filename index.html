Methodology

Settle The Nervous System Description

Create a calm, safe start so her body stops bracing and "locking up." We achieve this through the initial intake, understanding her goals and any injuries, prescribing a beginners class that involves breathing and activation of deep muscles.

Decompress & Align Description

Releasing tight muscles, and strengthening the weak ones. Through Pilates reformer we release tight areas (neck, shoulders, back) and start rebuilding the ideal posture.

Core That Holds Description

We strengthen the deep core muscles, so her posture doesn't collapse the moment she's tired or stressed.

FAQ's

I have an injury (neck, back, or knee). Is Pilates safe for me?

Yes. Pilates is gentle on the joints and focuses on controlled, safe movement. We offer classes clearly labeled for beginners and injured clients, and our instructors guide you to move safely.

I've never done Pilates before. Can beginners join?

Yes, absolutely! Our beginner classes are designed specifically for those with no prior experience. We break down every movement step-by-step.

Will the instructor watch and guide me during class?

Yes, our instructors provide close supervision and personal corrections throughout the class to ensure your posture is perfect and safe.

I'm afraid of the Pilates machines. What if I don't know how to use them?

There's no need to worry. Our team will introduce you to the reformer and other machines, setting them up properly for your body before you start.
